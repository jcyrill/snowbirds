# 1990s Cultural Research

## Technology

### Communication
- **Landline phones** - everyone had one, no caller ID early 90s
- **Pagers/Beepers** (1990-1999) - 911 meant call back urgently
- **Pay phones** - still everywhere, cost 25-35 cents
- **Answering machines** - tape-based, could screen calls
- **Caller ID** - became common mid-90s
- **Cell phones** - expensive, bulky (Motorola RAZR late 90s)
- **Car phones** - status symbol

### Internet & Computing
- **Dial-up internet** - screeching modem sounds, tied up phone line
- **AOL** - "You've Got Mail", chat rooms, instant messenger (AIM)
- **Email** - still novel, not everyone had it
- **Netscape Navigator** - dominant browser
- **Windows 95/98** - revolutionary at the time
- **No Google** - used Yahoo, AltaVista, Ask Jeeves
- **Slow downloads** - songs took 20+ minutes
- **Floppy disks** - 3.5" for storage

### Entertainment Media
- **VHS tapes** - renting movies, recording TV
- **Blockbuster Video** - Friday/Saturday night ritual
- **CDs** - replaced cassettes, $15-20 each
- **Mixtapes** - on cassette, later burned CDs
- **MTV** - still played music videos
- **Must-See TV** - appointment viewing, Thursday nights NBC

## Dating & Social Culture

### Pre-Digital Dating
- **Meeting in person** - bars, clubs, work, school, through friends
- **Personal ads** - newspaper classifieds
- **Phone calls** - had to call house phone, might talk to parents first
- **Video dating services** - VHS tapes of people introducing themselves
- **Speed dating** - became popular late 90s
- **No texting** - had to actually call and talk
- **Pager codes** - 143 = I love you, 07734 upside down = hello

### Social Dynamics
- **Bar culture** - major social scene
- **Coffee shop culture** - Starbucks expansion, Central Perk influence
- **Smoking** - still allowed in many bars/restaurants
- **Clubbing** - raves, techno/house music scene
- **No social media** - couldn't stalk someone online first
- **Photo development** - took film to store, waited days/week
- **Breakups were harder** - couldn't check their online activity
