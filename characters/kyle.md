## Personality

**Core Traits:**  
1. Good-natured but easily led
2. Dumber than Zack (but sweeter)
3. More naturally empathetic

**Strengths:**  
- Genuinely kind when not influenced by Zack
- Doesn't have malice in him
- Athletic and capable
- Open-minded and adaptable
- Makes friends easily (when being himself)
- Better listener than Zack

**Flaws:**  
- No independent identity (just follows Zack)
- Doesn't think things through (at all)
- Conflict-averse (goes along to get along)
- Lazy about developing own opinions
- Afraid to disagree with his brother
- Immature even for his age

**Sense of Humor:**  
Goofy physical comedy. Laughs at everything. More "Wayne's World" than "Beavis and Butt-Head." Doesn't get jokes right away. Unintentionally funny.

## Background

**Family:**  
Same wealthy family, but as younger brother he's gotten even less discipline. Zack gets expectations, Kyle gets indulgence. Parents love him but don't take him as seriously.

**Education:**  
Same private schools. Worse grades than Zack. Barely graduating. Probably had to be tutored. Social not academic. Popular because he's funny and harmless.

**Formative Experience:**  
Has always followed Zack. Doesn't know who he is without his brother. Never had to figure it out.

**Current Life Situation:**  
Living Zack's life one step behind. Happy to be along for the ride. College wasn't his idea - parents' expectation. No clue what he actually wants.

---

## Goals & Motivations

**External Goal (What they WANT):**  
Same as Zack initially - win money, get to college, make parents happy, have fun. But he's less driven about it.

**Internal Need (What they NEED):**  
Find his own identity separate from Zack. Learn to make his own decisions. Discover what HE actually wants vs. what others want for him. Grow up in his own way.

**Fears:**  
- Being without Zack
- Making wrong decisions alone
- Disappointing everyone
- Actually being dumb (not just playing dumb)
- Growing apart from his brother

**Secrets:**  
- Not sure he wants to go to college
- Sometimes exhausted by Zack's energy/plans
- Lonely even when surrounded by people
- Wishes someone would ask what HE wants

---

## Relationships

**With Zack:**  
Worships his brother. Follows his lead always. Their separation/growth is key to his arc. Will have to learn to stand up to him, not reject him.

**With Parents:**  
Wants to please them but doesn't understand how. Feels like the "other" brother - less pressure but also less respect. Needs their validation more than Zack does.

**With Grandparents:**  
Actually curious about them (more than Zack is). Might have clearer memories of visits. More open to connection.

**With The Girls:**  
Falls hard and fast. More genuine in his feelings than Zack initially. Wears heart on sleeve. His girl might see his potential before he does.

**With Elderly Community:**  
More naturally comfortable with them. Less judgment, more curiosity. They probably adopt him as grandson-figure. Enjoys hearing their stories.

---

## Character Arc

**Starting Point:**  
Good-hearted follower with no identity of his own. Goes along with Zack's bad behavior because he doesn't know how to be separate. Immature but not malicious.

**Transformation:**  
Florida separation from their normal world forces him to make own choices. Elderly people treat him as individual, not "Zack's brother." Girl likes HIM specifically. Has to make moral choice independent of Zack.

**Ending Point:**  
Still loves and supports his brother, but has own voice. Makes own decisions. Knows who he is. Still goofy, but authentically so. Can disagree with Zack. Partners, not leader/follower.
