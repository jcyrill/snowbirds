## ACT 1 - LAKE TAHOE (Pages 1-25)

### Setup - Rich Kids Paradise
- **Location:** San Francisco wealth â†’ Lake Tahoe summer house
- **Characters:** Zack & Kyle, rich party boys from SF
- **Lifestyle:** Wakeboarding with crazy wake boat, drinking, womanizing, zero responsibility
- **Goals:** Get to party colege
- **Activitie** Working on "ultimate trick" for future competition
- **Problems:** No jobs, don't care about future or values, everything handed to them

### Inciting Incident
- The retirement community hosts a lakeside luncheon honoring a beloved resident's pet cat (a furry mascot of the village). The cat's ashes are about to be scattered into Tahoe.
- Zack & Kyle, drunk and partying nearby, get too close to the dock and splash elderly guests while mocking them.
- In a desperate attempt to show off the "ultimate trick," they crash their wake boat into the ceremonial setup, knocking the urn into the lake along with the memorial display and expensive auction items.
- The cat's ashes are lost in the water, the seniors are soaked/injured, and the community is humiliated.

### Consequences
- Parents find out, costs lots of money to fix
- Parents use the boys' COLLEGE FUND to pay for damages
- **Ultimatum:** Figure out college on your own OR join the military
- **Punishment:** Shipped to live with grandparents in Florida for rest of summer
  - Can't get in any more trouble
  - Parents cut them off financially

### Act 1 Break (Page 25)
- Brothers arrive in Florida, everything changes

### Antagonists Arrive

  - Can build luxury condos, make millions
  
- **Corrupt retirement admin** 
  - In on the scam
  - Stopping residents' fees from being paid properly
  - Creates obstacles for brothers

- **Developer's two sons**
  - ALSO rich dicks who wakeboard
  - ALSO chasing the same girls
  - Everything Zack & Kyle were at beginning
  - Will compete at Miami contest

### Rising Conflicts
- Brothers trying to:
  - Maintain disguises
  - Train for competition
  - Romance the girls

  - Avoid getting caught again
  - Uncover the developer's scheme

### Love vs. Lies
- Girls start to really like them
- But brothers are lying about disguises
- Growing guilt about deception

### All Is Lost (Page 75)

- **Lose boat access** (kicked out of facility)
